Our Story

The Hungry Pub started out as a passion project for myself to show the
world my love for food. A place where you can sit down, have a drink and watch
the game. Nothing beats what we have to offer when it comes to food. With
nothing but the best locally sourced ingredients and ever changing menu; you
can always be sure to find something you love at The Hungry Pub. Feel free to stop
by and try a beer from one of our partnered microbreweries and watch the game on
one of our 30 TV's.
